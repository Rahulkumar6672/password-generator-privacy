Welcome to NXT Password. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring complete transparency about our data practices.

Our app does NOT collect, transmit, or store:

3. How the App Works

NXT Password is a completely offline application that operates entirely on your device:

  • Password Generation: All passwords are generated locally on your device using cryptographically secure random algorithms
  • Favorites Storage: Saved passwords are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences (local storage only)
  • No Internet Required: The app works completely offline and does not require any internet connectivity
  • No Servers: We do not operate any servers, databases, or cloud storage to store your data
  • No Data Transmission: No data ever leaves your device

4. Local Data Storage

The app stores favorite passwords locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences API. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is never transmitted to any external server
  • Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Can be manually cleared by clearing the app's data in Android settings
  • Is not backed up to cloud services

10. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. To delete all data stored by the app:

  1. Open Android Settings
  2. Go to Apps or Application Manager
  3. Find and select "NXT Password"
  4. Tap "Storage" or "Storage & cache"
  5. Tap "Clear data" or "Clear storage"

Alternatively, uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data including saved passwords.
